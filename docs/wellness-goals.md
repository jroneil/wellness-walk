# Wellness Goals

Goals are optional and disabled by default. Users choose weekly walking minutes,
completed-walk count, minimum qualifying duration, week start, and timezone. No
medical guideline is assumed.

Weekly minutes include explicitly completed and partially completed activities.
Walk count includes those activities only when their duration meets the configured
minimum. Skipped, dismissed, expired, unknown, and active records never contribute.
The week is calculated in the stored timezone from the configured start weekday.

The UI uses neutral language: completed walks, walking time, remaining minutes,
and active days. It does not show failure states, streak-loss warnings, rankings,
calories, or medical claims.

Completion quality and notes are optional reflections and do not alter scoring or goal targets. Completed and partially completed durations continue to use the documented qualification rules; skipped, dismissed, expired, and unknown opportunities do not contribute.
